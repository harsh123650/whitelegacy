How to Subscribe

Step 1: Navigate to the “Subscribe” tab on the White Legacy website.
Step 2: Fill in the subscription form with accurate details including your name, mobile number, address, and how many litres of milk you require daily.
Step 3: Once submitted, your request will be reviewed by our team for verification.
Step 4: Within 24 hours, you will receive a text message on your registered mobile number containing your Login ID and Password.
Step 5: Visit the White Legacy website and log in using your provided credentials.
Step 6: Proceed to the “Subscription” section and complete your payment securely to activate your subscription.

If you have any issues or need help, please reach out to our support team through the “Contact Us” section. We're here to help!
